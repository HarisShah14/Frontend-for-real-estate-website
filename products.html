Beautiful Park Estate 5BR Villa @ Riffa Views


Property type : Villa                 bedrooms : 4
property size : 333sqm            bathrooms : 5


Description

This gorgeous Park Estate Special Corner Villa comprising 5 bedrooms located in the Riffa Views, one of the most sought-after developments in Bahrain. Riffa Views offers residents a peaceful and idyllic location to live a relaxing life, but is still conveniently located to shops, hospitals, restaurants, and an international school, as well as being well connected to road networks. All this with a championship golf course in your back garden.
... The Park Estate is an exclusive enclave within Riffa Views providing the sophisticated Mediterranean lifestyle which includes Green vast parks and walkways, palm tree-lined avenues, exotic residences that reflect the brilliant sun with their earth-colored shades, and flared gardens that bring the land to life. Every element is carefully designed to accentuate the rich Mediterranean lifestyle which is clearly reflected in the architecture of its town-houses and villas.
